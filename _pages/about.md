Jilin Hu is a Full Professor at the School of Data Science and Engineering, East China Normal University. His research develops data management and machine learning methods for spatio-temporal data, urban mobility, time series, and AI for science, with an emphasis on models that are both effective and practical.

Before joining ECNU, he was an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Aalborg University and a Research Associate at the Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence under the supervision of [Prof. Jianbing Shen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=_Q3NTToAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). He received his Ph.D. from Aalborg University in 2019, supervised by [Prof. Christian S. Jensen](http://people.cs.aau.dk/~csj/) and [Prof. Bin Yang](http://people.cs.aau.dk/~byang/). From Oct. 2017 to Apr. 2018, he visited the University of California, Berkeley, supervised by [Prof. Alexandre Bayen](https://www2.eecs.berkeley.edu/Faculty/Homepages/bayen.html).
