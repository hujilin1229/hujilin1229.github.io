Jilin Hu is a Full Professor at the School of Data Science and Engineering, East China Normal University. His research develops data management and machine learning methods for spatio-temporal data, urban mobility, time series, and AI for science.

Before joining ECNU, he was an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Aalborg University and a Research Associate at the Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence under the supervision of [Prof. Jianbing Shen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=_Q3NTToAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). He received his Ph.D. from Aalborg University in 2019, supervised by [Prof. Christian S. Jensen](http://people.cs.aau.dk/~csj/) and [Prof. Bin Yang](http://people.cs.aau.dk/~byang/). From Oct. 2017 to Apr. 2018, he visited the University of California, Berkeley, supervised by [Prof. Alexandre Bayen](https://www2.eecs.berkeley.edu/Faculty/Homepages/bayen.html).

#### Research Interests

<ul class="research-list">
  <li><strong>Spatio-temporal data management</strong><span>Indexing, querying, learning, and reasoning over spatial and temporal data.</span></li>
  <li><strong>Traffic data analytics</strong><span>Trajectory modeling, map inference, demand forecasting, and intelligent transportation systems.</span></li>
  <li><strong>Time series and lightweight ML</strong><span>Efficient models for forecasting, compression, representation learning, and deployment.</span></li>
  <li><strong>AI for science</strong><span>Machine learning for molecular, material, and scientific data analysis.</span></li>
</ul>
